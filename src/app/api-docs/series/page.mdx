# 合集

合集（系列）的创建、管理和剧集排序接口。

---

## 合集列表

<Endpoint method="POST" path="series.list" auth="public" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="limit" type="number" required>每页数量</P>
  <P name="page" type="number" required>页码</P>
  <P name="sortBy" type="string">排序方式</P>
</ParamTable>

---

## 合集详情

<Endpoint method="POST" path="series.getById" auth="public" />

返回合集信息及所含剧集列表。

<ParamTable>
  <P name="id" type="string" required>合集 ID</P>
</ParamTable>

---

## 创建合集

<Endpoint method="POST" path="series.create" auth="protected" scope="content:write" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="title" type="string" required>合集标题</P>
  <P name="description" type="string">描述</P>
  <P name="coverUrl" type="string">封面 URL</P>
  <P name="downloadUrl" type="string">下载链接</P>
  <P name="downloadNote" type="string">下载说明</P>
</ParamTable>

## 管理剧集

### 添加视频到合集

<Endpoint method="POST" path="series.addVideo" auth="protected" scope="content:write" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="seriesId" type="string" required>合集 ID</P>
  <P name="videoId" type="string" required>视频 ID</P>
  <P name="episodeNum" type="number">集数编号</P>
  <P name="episodeTitle" type="string">集数标题</P>
</ParamTable>

### 从合集移除视频

<Endpoint method="POST" path="series.removeVideo" auth="protected" scope="content:write" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="seriesId" type="string" required>合集 ID</P>
  <P name="videoId" type="string" required>视频 ID</P>
</ParamTable>

### 重新排序剧集

<Endpoint method="POST" path="series.reorderEpisodes" auth="protected" scope="content:write" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="seriesId" type="string" required>合集 ID</P>
  <P name="episodes" type="object[]" required>剧集排序 `[{videoId, episodeNum}]`</P>
</ParamTable>
