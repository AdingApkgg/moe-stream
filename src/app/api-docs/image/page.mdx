# 图片

图片帖的发布、查询和互动操作接口。

---

## 获取图片列表

<Endpoint method="POST" path="image.list" auth="public" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="limit" type="number" required>每页数量</P>
  <P name="page" type="number" required>页码</P>
  <P name="search" type="string">搜索关键词</P>
  <P name="tagSlugs" type="string[]">标签 slug 筛选</P>
  <P name="sortBy" type="string">排序：`latest` / `views`</P>
</ParamTable>

---

## 获取图片详情

<Endpoint method="POST" path="image.getById" auth="public" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="id" type="string" required>图片帖 ID</P>
</ParamTable>

---

## 创建图片帖

<Endpoint method="POST" path="image.create" auth="protected" scope="content:write" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="title" type="string" required>标题（1-200 字符）</P>
  <P name="images" type="string[]" required>图片 URL 数组（至少一张）</P>
  <P name="description" type="string">描述（最长 5000 字符）</P>
  <P name="tagNames" type="string[]">标签名称数组</P>
</ParamTable>

<CopyBlock>{`curl -X POST 'https://your-domain.com/api/trpc/image.create' \\
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \\
  -H 'Authorization: Bearer sk-your-api-key' \\
  -d '{"json":{"title":"图片标题","images":["https://example.com/img1.jpg","https://example.com/img2.jpg"],"tagNames":["标签1"]}}'`}</CopyBlock>
