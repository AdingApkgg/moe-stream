# 视频

视频内容的增删改查、互动操作、浏览记录、推荐等接口。

---

## 获取视频列表

<Endpoint method="POST" path="video.list" auth="public" />

分页查询已发布的视频，支持标签筛选和搜索。

<ParamTable>
  <P name="limit" type="number" required>每页数量（1-50，默认 20）</P>
  <P name="page" type="number" required>页码（从 1 开始）</P>
  <P name="search" type="string">搜索关键词</P>
  <P name="tagSlugs" type="string[]">标签 slug 筛选</P>
  <P name="excludeTagSlugs" type="string[]">排除的标签 slug</P>
  <P name="sortBy" type="string">排序：`latest`（默认）/ `views` / `likes`</P>
  <P name="timeRange" type="string">时间范围筛选</P>
</ParamTable>

## 创建视频

<Endpoint method="POST" path="video.create" auth="protected" scope="content:write" />

<ParamTable>
  <P name="title" type="string" required>视频标题（1-100 字符）</P>
  <P name="videoUrl" type="string" required>视频文件 URL</P>
  <P name="description" type="string">描述（最长 5000 字符）</P>
  <P name="coverUrl" type="string">封面图 URL</P>
  <P name="tagNames" type="string[]">标签名称数组（自动匹配或创建）</P>
  <P name="tagIds" type="string[]">标签 ID 数组</P>
  <P name="duration" type="number">视频时长（秒）</P>
  <P name="extraInfo.author" type="string">作者名称</P>
  <P name="extraInfo.downloads" type="object[]">下载链接 `[{name, url, password?}]`</P>
  <P name="extraInfo.keywords" type="string[]">关键词数组</P>
</ParamTable>

<CopyBlock>{`curl -X POST 'https://your-domain.com/api/trpc/video.create' \\
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \\
  -H 'Authorization: Bearer sk-your-api-key' \\
  -d '{"json":{"title":"视频标题","videoUrl":"https://example.com/video.mp4","description":"描述","coverUrl":"https://example.com/cover.jpg","tagNames":["标签1","标签2"],"extraInfo":{"author":"作者","downloads":[{"name":"网盘","url":"https://pan.example.com/xxx","password":"1234"}]}}}'`}</CopyBlock>
